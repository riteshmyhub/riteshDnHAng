Mild Steel Electrodes

CELLUTHERME®

AWS: E6010    IS: EC4310X

CELLUTHERME is a medium heavy rutile coated mild steel electrode designed for welding of unalloyed structural steels in all positions. The electrode gives smooth radiographic quality weld with low spatter and smoke. Slag is self detachable and the bead shape is uniform and finely rippled.
