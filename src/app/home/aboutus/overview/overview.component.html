D&H Sécheron Overview

D&H Sécheron is one of the leading welding manufacturers in India.


Incepted in 1966, D&H Sécheron is an ISO 9001-2015 certified company that offers a comprehensive

range of welding solutions in the form of consumables, equipments, training and fabrication.


D&H Sécheron has been constantly challenging the status quo in the welding space with their

state-of-the-art production facilities, engineering prowess and wide spread dealer network, thus,

bringing forth the core values of Quality, Innovation and Import Substitution into existence.
