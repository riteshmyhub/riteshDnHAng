About D&H Sécheron

D&H Sécheron is one of the leading welding manufacturers in India.

Incepted in 1966, D&H Sécheron is an ISO 9001-2015 certified company thatoffers a comprehensive range of welding solutions in the form of consumables,equipments, training and fabrication.

D&H Sécheron has been constantly challenging the status quo in the welding space with their state-of-the-art production facilities, engineering prowess andwide spread dealer network, thus, bringing forth the core values of Quality,Innovation and Import Substitution into existence.
