Power Sector


The Power Sector though most important also happens to be the most wear prone one leaving room

for factors like abrasion, impact, erosion, corrosion, cavitation and other breakdowns to affect the

critical components required for power generation. Replacement of worn out components is an

expensive and time consuming proposition as it needs plant stoppage in many cases. Our LoTherme

Research and development (R&D) offers a series of solutions to combat the wear & tear with minimal

cost so that the components in a power plant run undeterred and uninterrupted
